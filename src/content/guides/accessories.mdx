## Power and charging

- **USB-C PD power bank (65W+)** to keep Steam Deck/Switch/iPad alive on flights.
- **Compact 65W+ wall charger** with interchangeable plugs for international trips.
- **Short USB-C cables** for lower resistance and tidier setups.

## Video and networking

- **USB-C hub or travel dock** with HDMI and Ethernet for hotel TVs.
- **Travel router** to convert hotel Ethernet to Wiâ€‘Fi and isolate your devices.
- **Flat HDMI cable** for tight hotel TV mounts.

## Controllers and input

- **Xbox / DualSense** for broad compatibility; **8BitDo** for compact travel.
- **Phone/tablet clips** or **kickstands** for better ergonomics.
- **Spare thumb grips** and **screen protectors** for handhelds.

## Carry and protection

- **Hard shell case** for handhelds; **slim sleeves** for tablets.
- **Cable organiser** to prevent tangles and lost adapters.
- **Folding stand** for iPad/Android tablets and Switch tabletop mode.

## Quick packing list

- PD power bank + wall charger  
- USB-C hub/dock + HDMI + Ethernet  
- Controller + stand/clip  
- Case + cable organiser + screen protector
