## Overview

**Cat Quest** is a cute, lightweight action RPG set in an open world full of cat puns, dungeons, and simple loot. It’s easy to learn, forgiving, and ideal for younger players or anyone who wants a more relaxed RPG. It’s also available on **Tesla Arcade** on some models. 

## Where you can play it

- **PC**
- **Nintendo Switch**
- **PlayStation / Xbox**
- **Mobile**
- **Tesla Arcade** on some cars

## Why it works on the move

- **Short quests and dungeons** – perfect for bite-sized sessions.
- **Friendly difficulty curve** – good for kids and casual players.
- **Runs well on low-power hardware** – great for handhelds.

## Play it on…

- **Switch / handheld consoles**
- **Steam Deck / handheld PCs**
- **Tesla** (as a fun surprise for passengers)
- **Mobile** if you want true pocket-play.
